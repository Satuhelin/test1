When Filipinos move overseas to work, they seldom come to the US

Filippinos are one of the biggest minorities in the US but still most of Filippinos working abroad at the moment live totally elsewhere.

Sources: Commission on Filipinos Overseas, Philippine Statistics Authority

Until 2000 Philippines was the biggest source of legal immigration from Asia tot the US. Now it is the fourth.

Source: Yearbook of Immigration Statistics
